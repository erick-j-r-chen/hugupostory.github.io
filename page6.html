虎姑婆 Aunt Tigress: A Taiwanese Folk Tale

What do you do

You hide in a tree outside your house, from which you can see the demon. From your vantage point, do you keep on hiding sneakily not to capture attention or...do you risk it all and shout to the demon? You decide it is now or never and shout to the demon: "I will be a good girl and jump into your mouth!" you holler.
