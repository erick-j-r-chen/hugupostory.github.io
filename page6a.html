虎姑婆 Aunt Tigress: A Taiwanese Folk Tale

What do you do

You cannot believe your luck. The little girl will become your prey! She then tells you to boil a pot of boiling water so that she can jump into the pot and become stew for you to eat. "I taste better as a stew" she hollers. You cannot believe how wonderful this feast will be. You boil a boiling pot of water and close your eyes before enjoying your almost-ready stew.

How good will your feast taste?
