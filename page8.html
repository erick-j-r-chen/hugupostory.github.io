虎姑婆: 台灣傳統故事 Aunt Tigress: A Taiwanese Folk Tale

What do you do

The demon is hungry again but asks "why do you want boiling water? come into my belly and be my next meal!" You reply from high above your tree: "I will taste much better in a stew! prepare some boiling water so I can turn into delicious stew!" "Hmmm sounds delicious, okay!" replies the stupid demon.
