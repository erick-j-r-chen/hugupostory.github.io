虎姑婆 Aunt Tigress: A Taiwanese Folk Tale

What do you do

You try to chase the young girl around but you become sleepy and tired after running around looking for her to eat for so long. A few minutes later however, you are woken up by the voice of the young girl. "I will be a good girl and jump into your mouth!" she hollers.

Do you believe her and patiently wait for your prey to come right into your hands?

You've eaten enough little children to know that they are sometimes quite cheeky. Could she possibly be trying to taunt you? To mock you and trick you?
