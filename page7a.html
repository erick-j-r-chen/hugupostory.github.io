虎姑婆 Aunt Tigress: A Taiwanese Folk Tale

What do you do

You close your eyes to get ready to eat your feast. However, you suddenly feel a burning sensation all over! You have never felt so much pain in your life! You struggle in your final moments to open your eyes to see the little girl laughing at you...you have been completely outsmarted and now it is your turn to become the next meal for the townspeople.
