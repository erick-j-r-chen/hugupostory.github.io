What do you do

After the demon boils the cauldron of boiling water, you ask the demon to hoist it up to your tree. "I will be a good girl and jump into the boiling pot myself and become stew!" The demon, who is increasingly becoming hungrier craves for stew and eagerly hoists the large cauldron of boiling water up to your tree. What's in store next for you?
